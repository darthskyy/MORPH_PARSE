Umsebenzi olandelayo uthatha eli nyathelo ulise phambili uchonge iiprojekthi nemisebenzi efuna ukusungulelwa ukuhlangabezana nesicwangciso-buqili ngasinye . Bonisanani nabelane ngezimvo malunga neendlela le mithetho-siseko enokufezekiswa ngcono ngayo . Jonga imibuzo ecetyisiweyo kuluhlu olukwiphepha 90 nelinokongeza kwizimvo ezinikwe ngabathathi-nxaxheba . I-Organised Local Government Act , 1997 inikezela ulwamkelo kumbutho omnye kazwelonke omele uninzi loomasipala kwiphondo ngalinye . I-South African Local Government Association ( SALGA ) sisigqeba esisemthethweni esaziwayo kurhulumente wengingqi . I-SALGA imele imiba exhalabisayo yamalungu ayo . Abathathi-nxaxheba baphendula imibuzo engezantsi ngokwamaqela ezithathu kwimizuzu embalwa baze babelane ngeempendulo kwindibano yabo bonke . Zintlobo zini zeengxaki ezifanele kukusonjululwa luhlahlo lwabiwo-mali ? Kubalulekile abantu bavakalise izimvo zabo babe negalelo , nokuba ngathi alubalulekanga kangakanani , njengoko oku kuya kuphucula ingqiqo nobunini . I-PMS kamasipala kufuneka iquke imiba ephawulwe libhunga njengoko inxulumene ngqo kwii-KPA zoomasipala balo . Ezi KPI zilandelayo kufuneka zibe yinxenye ye-PMS kamasipala ngamnye de zibe ziya setyenziswa ngulo masipala . Ezi zizalathisi zikazwelonke ezinikezelwe nguMphathiswa emva kothethwano nee-MEC zephondo kurhulumente wengingqi ne-SALGA . Kodwa ke , kubalulekile ukunakana ukuba ukungafani kukwafuna ukubonwa kwimiba enxulumene negunya nezibonelelo . Okuninzi koko kunakanwa ' njengokungafani ' kuvele ngenxa yonxulumano lwegunya nemicimbi yokwabiwa kwezibonelelo . Iiyantlukwano azimiswa ngenx'engaphambili zonke , kwaye nalapho kwenzekayo , zenziwa nkulu ngezinye izinyanzelo zezentlalo nezoqoqosho . Kwiimeko ezininzi iiyantlukwano phakathi kwesityebi nehlwempu ; ukuqeshwa nokungaqeshwa ; iindawo ezisedolophini namaphandle ; ofundileyo nongafundanga zakhiwe ngokwasentlalweni kwaye zinopolitiko ngokunzulu . Ezi yantlukwano zinokuncinci okunokwenza nezimo zomzimba azalwe nazo umntu kwaye ulawulo lokungafani oluyimpumelelo luya kufuna ingqwalasela ngokusekeke kwinkcubeko yamalungelo oluntu . I-PMS INAKO UKUSEBENZA NJENGESIXHOBO ESILUMKISA KUSELITHUBA NANJENGOKO IBONISA UKUNGASEBENZI OKANYE UKUSEBENZA NGOKUNGEKHO MGANGATHWENI , NGAKO OKO IVUMELA UNGENELELO UKUZE KUBE KHO UKUPHUCUKA . Enye yeenjongo eziphambili zikarhulumente kukudlulisela uxanduva lokunikezela iinkonzo evela kurhulumente kazwelonke ukuya kowamaphondo nakoosomagunya bengingqi . Ukunikezelwa kweenkonzo kufuneka kube sezandleni zabo bakufutshane nabasebenzisi kunakurhulumente omkhulu , yena yena ' emgceni ongaphambili ' . Kufuneka ibe ibe nesakhelo somgaqo-nkqubo kazwelonke ophuhliswe ngamasebe abandakanyekayo karhulumente kazwelonke . Urhulumente kufuneka aphucule ulawulo lwakhe lwesixokelelwano , kuquka izivumelwano zokungenelela phakathi koomasipala bengingqi nabasekhaya apho kukho ukuphindwaphindwa okanye ungquzulwano ekwabiweni koxanduva nemithombo . Amaphondo kumele ajolise kwimisebenzi yawo engundoqo nokuphuhlisa isikhundla ukuxhasa nokongamela urhulumente wasekhaya . Ubume bomgaqo-siseko buvumela ukuba kubekho ukwahluka ngakumbi ekunikezelweni kwamandla nemisebenzi , kwaye oku kumele kusetyenziswe ukuqinisekisa ukuthi gingci okungcono phakathi kwesikhundla noxanduva lwamaphondo noomasipala . Inkqubo ekhoyo ingaphuculwa , ngengcaciso yoxanduva kwiinkalo zezindlu , zamanzi , zogutyulo , zombane nothutho loluntu . Iidolophukazi ezinkulu kumele zinikwe imali karhulumente enkulu namandla opolitiko ekuququzeleleni ukuphuculwa kokuhlaliswa koluntu , uthutho nokucwangciswa kwesithuba . Kwezinye iinkalo , izinto zommandla zingabonelela iinkonzo egameni loomasipala abangenamithombo ingephi , kodwa oku kumele kukhokelwe ngoomasipala ukuphepha ukujongelwa phantsi kokunika ingxelo ngokwentando yesininzi kuhanjiso lweenkonzo . Chaza iimpendulo zawo kumsebenzi weqela . Isiqinisekiso se-ISO : ukuba nexhala lokuphumeza inkqubo eyamkelweyo kumazwe ngamazwe ngokuphathelele kulawulo lobulunga , ukuzihlola novavanyo lweqela lesithathu lomvelisi . Imvelaphi yangaphandle : Ezi zenziwa ngaphandle kukamasipala njengemali-mboleko efunyenwe kumaziko orhwebo . Ukulawula ixesha : Ukuyiqonda indlela ehamba ngayo iprojekthi nokudibana rhoqo namaqela achaphazelekayo neqela leprojekthi ngeenjongo zokujonga imiba ephathelele kwixesha . Zininzi izinto ezinokwenza ukuba ixesha ebelibekelwe iprojekthi litshintshe . Izinto ezinokuthi zivele kufuneka zilungiselelwe kwaphaya ekuqaleni kokubekwa kwamaxesha , kwaye onke amaqela achaphazelekayo kufuneka aziswe ngenkqubo . Ubulunga bemfundo kuninzi lwabantwana abamnyama buphantsi . Oku kwalela abafundi abaninzi ukufikelela kwingqesho . Kukwanciphisa ubunakho bemivuzo nokuhamba kwezemfundo kwabo baye bawufumane umsebenzi - ze kunyine ubunakho bamandla bamashishini oMzantsi Afrika . Ishedyuli yezibonelelo iya kuqulatha imisebenzi ebithe yaqulunqwa kwishedyuli yemisebenzi , kwaye ke le misebenzi kufuneka idweliswe kwakhona kwakunye nazo zonke iimfuno neendleko ehamba nazo ngeenjongo zokuqinisekisa ukufezekiswa kwayo .
