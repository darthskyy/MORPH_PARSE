Abahlanganyeli benza isibonelo esingenzasi sokobana i-KPI ihlangabezana bunjani neemfuneko zemithetjhwana . Sebenzisa umuda wokuthoma njengesibonelo bese iinqhema ziqedelela ithebula . Umlando weSewula Afrika namalemuko weenarha ezinye ukhombisa bonyana ubunye nebumbano lomphakathi kuzizinto eziqakathekileko ekuphumeleliseni iminqopho yezehlalakuhle nomnotho . Isethulo somThethosisekelo simumethe isilinganiswesi ngendlela efaneleko . Samukela isibopho sombuso sokubuyisela emuva imiphumela yebandlululo , ngendlela ezakwenza bonyana zoke izakhamuzi zizizwe ziyingcenye yenarha kanye namahlelo wayo . AmaKomidi weWadi kufanele athuthukise bekafake neendlela ezizokusiza ekudluliseleni ilwazi elinqophileko emphakathini nokusuka emkhandlwini ukuya emphakathini . Iinkambiso kufanele zitjheje amahlelo kamasipala kanye namaphrojekthi nokubona bona kungagcugcuzeleka bunjani ukuthintana okuzokubandakanya ukuhlanganyela komphakathi . Ukunikela izakhamuzi zendawo ithuba lokungena ematjhebiswaneni nemakontrageni norhulumende wendawo ukuze bahlele ezinye iinsetjenziswa ezingeziweko . Amaqhinga mitlamo ekufuneka bonyana kusetjenzwe ngayo ukuphumelelisa umnqopho . Emsebenzini olandelako amaqhinga ayakhonjwa ukwenzela bonyana kwakhelwe phezu kwamakghono begodu kupheliswe ubuthakathaka . Umsebenzi olandelako uragelisa phambili igadangweli begodu ukhomba amaphrojekthi nemisebenzi ekufuze isungulwe ukusebenza ngqhinga elinye nelinye . Ukucocisana ngemiraro begodu nabelane imibono ngeendlela iinkambisolawulo zingaphumelela ngcono ngayo . Dlulisela emibuzweni ephakanyisiwe ethebulini elisekhasini lama-90 elingezelela imibono enikelwe bahlanganyeli . UmThetho kaRhulumende weKhaya oHleliweko , we-1997 unikela ilemukiso ehlanganweni kazweloke ejamela abomasipala abanengi esifundeni . IHlangano kaRhulumende weKhaya yeSewula Afrika ( SALGA ) yihlangano elimukisa ngokusemthethweni mayelana norhulumende wekhaya . I-PMS ijamele iinghonghoyilo zamalungayo . Abahlanganyeli baphendula imibuzo engaphasi ngeenqhema zamalunga amathathu imizuzu embalwa bese babelana iimpendulo emhlanganweni wamalunga woke . Ngiyiphi imiraro ekufuze ithintwe / isonjululwe yibhajedi ? Kuqakathekile bonyana abantu baveze imibono yabo begodu bafake isandla , nanyana eminye imibono ingabonakala inga ayikaqakatheki , kodwana ingakwazi ukwenza bonyana abantu bezwisise ngcono begodu lokhu bakuthathe njengomsebenzi owenziwe ngibo . I-PMS kamasipala kufanele ifake hlangana iindaba ezikhonjiwe yikhansela njengobana lihlobana bunqopha nama-KPA wabomasipala babo . Ama-KPI alandelako kufanele abe yingcenye ye-PMS kamasipala omunye nomunye ngendlela yokobana benzeka kuye umasipala loyo . Lezi ziinkomba ezikhutjhwa nguNgqoqgotjhe ngemva kokubonisana no-MEC wesifunda mayelana norhulumende wekhaya ne-SALGA . Kodwana , kuqakathekile ukukhumbula bonyana ukuhlukana kubuye kudinge ukubonwa ezintweni ezihlobana namandla neentlabagelo . Okunengi kwalokhu kumele kukhunjulwe njengokuhlukana kuvele ngebanga lobudlelwano bamandla neendaba zokwetjelwana kweentlabagelo . Iindlela zokwehlukana akusizo zoke ezikhethwa ngaphambili kwesikhathi ngokomzimba , begodu nanyana kungaba kukuphi lapha zikhona zenziwa zibe zikulu ngeendlela ezinye eziphathelene nomphakathi begodu nanyana kungaba kukuphi lapha zikhona zenziwa zibe nomphakathi begodu neenjamiso zokomnotho . Eenkhathini ezinengi umehluko phakathi kweenjinga neenchaka ; abasebenzako nabangasebenziko ; emadorobheni nemakhaya ; abafundileko nabangakafundi baneenjamiso eziphathelene nomphakathi begodu nokutjhinga nokutjhinga okubupolotiki . Ukwehluka nokuthintana kancani nemikghwa ephathelene nomzimba okungathi ngayo umuntu ngamunye abelethwe begodu nokuphathwa kokuhlukana okuphumelelako kuzakutlhogeka itjhejo elidzimelele endleleni yamalungelo wobuntu . UMASIPALA NGAMUNYE NGOKOMTHETHO KUTLHOGEKA BONA UTHUTHUKISE IKAMBISO YOKUHLOLA UMSEBENZI ( I-PMS ) EZAKWENZA NGCONO UKUHLELEKA NOKUSEBENZA KUHLE KWEHLANGANO , UKUZIPHENDULELA mayelana NOKUSETJENZISWA KWEENSETJENZISWA ZAKAMASIPALA BESE KUVEZWA UKUZUZWA KWEMIPHUMELA . I-PMS OKHUNYE GODU IYAKGHONA UKUSEBENZA NJENGENDLELA YESIYELELISO ESIMSINYA NJENGOMBANA IVEZA UKUNGASEBENZI NANYANA UKUSEBENZA NGOKUNGASI SEZINGENI ELIFANELEKO , NGIKHO NJE KUVUNYELWA UKUNGENA HLANGANA OKUFANELEKO mayelana NOKWENZA NGCONO . Munye weminqopho karhulumende bekukudlulisela umsebenzi mayelana nokwenziwa komsebenzi usuke kurhulumende welizwe uye kurhulumende wamaphrovinsi neemphathiswa zendawo . Ukwenziwa komsebenzi kufanele kube sezandleni zalabo abatjhidelene abatjhidelene nabenzelwa umsebenzi kunorhulumende ophakathi , isiwe kude ukusuka kilokho okumelwe ' kuthonywe ngakho ' . Kufanele nanyana kunjalo uwele hlangana nemigomo elitlhatlha yelizwe ethuthukiswe minyango karhulumende welizwe efaneleko . Umleyo womthethosisekelo uvumela ukuhlukanisa ekwabeni amandla nemisebenzi , begodu lokhu kufanele kusetjenziswe ukuqinisekisa bonyana kuba khona ukukhambelana phakathi kwamakghono neembopho zeemfunda nabomasipala . Irherho elikhona lingenziwa ngcono , ngokuhlathululwa kweembopho zomkhakha wokwakhiwa kwezindlu , wamanzi , wehlwengiso , wegezi neenthuthi zomphakathi . Amadorobha amakhulu kufanele anikelwe imali enengana yokuthuthukisa umphakathi kanye namandla wepolotiki ukuhlanganisa ukwenziwa ngcono kokuhlaliswa kwabantu , komtlamo weenthuthi nokuhlaliswa kwabantu eendaweni ezilungele ukuhlala . Kweminye imikhakha , iinsiza zeemphande zingaletha isizo esikhundleni sabomasipala abanemithombo enganeliko , kodwana lokhu kufanele kurholwe bomasipala ukubalekela ukweqiswa kwamehlo kwendaba yokuziphendulela mayelana nokwenziwa kwemisebenzi . Ngonobangela wesikhathi amapara anganikelwa indatjana eyodwa namtjhana ezimbili bona azitjheje . I-akhredithetjhini ye-ISO : okumayelana nokwenziwa kwekambiso yelizwe loke egunyaziweko mayelana nekhwalithi yokuphatha kanye nokuhlolwa komuntu oseqadi webubulo . Imithombo yangaphandle : Le ikhiqizwa ngaphandle kwakamasipala njengemali ebolekwa eendaweni ezifana neenhlangano zamarhwebo . Ukulawula isikhathi : ukutjheja iragelo phambili lephrojekthi begodu nokuhlangana njalo nababambisani kanye namalunga wesiqhema sephrojekthi ukumeda nokuhlola izinto ezenzeka ngesikhathi esithile . Abonobangela abanengi bangabangela isikhathi sephrojekthi bona sitjhuguluke . Okusameleko kufanele kwakhelwe etjhejulini yokuthoma begodu iragelo phambili kufanele likhulunyiswe kibo boke ababambisani . Ikhwalithi yefundo yenengi labantwana abanzima iyabogaboga . Lokhu kukhandela inengi labafundi bonyana lingakghoni ukufumana imisebenzi . Begodu kuphungula amandla womrholo kanye nokuthuthuka ngokwebizelo kwalabo abafumana imisebenzi - begodu kukhawula amandla wokukhula kwamabhizinisi weSewula Afrika . Itjhejuli yomsebenzi imumatha imisebenzi ebuthelelwe etjhejulini yemisebenzi , lokhu kufanele kutlolwe godu kanye neemfuneko neendleko ezikhambelanako ukuqinisekisa ukuphumelela kwayo .
