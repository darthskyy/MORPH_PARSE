Bonisanani nabelane nangemibono mayelana nezindlela okungafinyelelwa ngazo kangcono kule migomo . Bheka imibuzo ephakanyisiwe kuthebula ekhasini lama-90 engangeza kule mibono ebekwe ngababambiqhaza . UMthetho Wohulumeni Bendawo Abahlelekile , ka-1997 unikeza ukwemukeleka kokuhleleka kuzwelonke okumele omasipala abaningi esifundazweni ngasinye . Inhlangano Yohulumeni Bendawo YaseNingizimu Afrika ( SALGA ) yisakhiwo esemukelekile sohulumeni bendawo . I-SALGA imele izimfuno zamalungu ayo . Ababambe iqhaza baphendula imibuzo engezansi emaqenjni abantu abathathu imizuzu embalwa bese kushiyelwana ngezimpendulo ekulungiseleleni . Yiziphi izinhlobo zezinkinga ukwaba imali okufanele kuzethule ? Kusemqoka ukunikeza abantu ithuba lokuveza izimvo zabo basize , ngisho noma ngabe abakushoyo kubukeka kungelutho , ngoba ngokwenze njalo kuphuculwa ukuqonda nokuzizwa bengabanikazi bohlelo . I-PMS kamasipala kufanele ifake nezindaba ezivezwe ngumkhandlu njengoba zihambisana nama-KPI omasipala wawo . Lawa ma-KPI alandelayo kufanele abe yingxenye ye-PMS kamasipala ngamunye ngendlela yokuthi ayahambisana nalowo masipala . Lezi yizinkomba zikazwelonke ezikhishwa nguNgqongqoshe ngemuva kokubonisana no-MEC bezifunda bohulumeni basekhaya ne-SALGA . Kodwa-ke , kubalulekile ukuvuma ukuthi ukwahlukahlukana kudinga futhi ukuthi kubonakale mayelana nezindaba ezihlobene namandla kanye nezinsiza . Okuningi okubonakala ' njengokwahlukahlukana ' kuvela ngenxa yezindaba ezimayelana namandla kanye nokwabiwa kwezinsiza . Ukwahlukana akuhlali kubonakala , futhi lapho okubonakala khona kwenziwa kube kukhulu ngokusebenzisa izakhiwo zomphakathi kanye nezomnotho . Ezimweni eziningi ukwahlukana phakathi kwabayizicebi kanye nabahlwempu ; abasebenzayo nabangasebenzi ; abasemadolobheni nabasemaphandleni ; abafundile nabangafundile ; kuyakhiwa emphakathini futhi kugxilile ngokwezombusazwe . Lokhu kwahlukana kunomthelela omncane emlandweni obonakalayo azalwa nawo umuntu futhi ukuphatha ukwahlukahlukana ngempumelelo kuyodinga ukubhekwa kususelwa esikweni lamalungelo esintu . I-PMS IYAKWAZI FUTHI UKUSEBENZA NJENGENDLELA YOKUXWAYISA YANGAPHAMBI KWESIKHATHI NJENGOBA IBONISA UKUNGASEBENZI NGEMFANELO NOMA UKUSEBENZA NGAPHANSI KWEZINGA ELIBEKIWE , NGALOKHO IVUMELA UKUNGENELELA OKUFANELE UKUZE KWENZIWE NGCONO . Eyodwa yezinhloso zikahulumeni ukudlulisela izibopho zokwethulwa kwezinsizakalo ukusuka kuHulumeni kazwelonke kuya kuziphathimandla zesifundazwe nezendawo . Ukwethulwa kwezinsizakalo kumele kube sezandleni zalabo abaseduzane kakhulu nabasebenzisi bokugcina kunokuba kuHulumeni waphakathi , okusuke kakhulu ' emugqeni wokuqala ' . Kumele kuphinde kuwele ngaphakathi kwezinhlaka zenqubomgomo kazwelonke ezithuthukiswe yiminyango kaHulumeni kazwelonke efanele . Uhlaka lomthethosisekelo luvumela ukwahluka ekwabiweni kwamandla nemisebenzi , lokhu kumele kwenziwe ukuqinisekisa ukusebenzisana kahle phakathi kwamakhono nemisebenzi yezifundazwe nomasipala . Indlela ekhona ingathuthukiswa , ngokuthi kucaciswe ngemisebenzi emikhakheni yezezindlu , ezamanzi , ezokususwa kokungcola , ezogesi kanye nezokuthuthwa komphakathi . Amadolobha amakhulu kumele anikezwe imali namandla ezombusazwe amaningi ukulawula ukuthuthukiswa kokuhlaliswa kwabantu , ezokuthutha nokuhlelwa kwezindawo . Kwezinye izindawo , imisebenzi yezifunda ingahlinzeka ngemisebenzi yenzele omasipala abangenazo kahle izinsizakusebenza , kodwa lokhu kumele kuholwe omasipala ukubalekela ukubukela phansi ukulethwa kwemisebenzi ngokukahulumeni wentando yeningi . Ngenxa yokungabibikho kwesikhathi esanele abantu ngababili kungenzeka banikezwe isigameko esisodwa noma ezimbili abazozibheka . Isiqinisekiso se-ISO : siphathelene nokusetshenziswa kwesimiso esamukelwe ezingeni lomhlaba sokuphatha okusezingeni elihle , ukuzihlola kanye nokuba umhloli wangaphandle kwebhizinisi ahlole umenzi womkhiqizo . Imithombo yangaphandle : Le mithombo yenziwa ngaphandle kukamasipala njengemalimboleko etholwa ezikhungwini zohwebo . Ukulawulwa kwesikhathi : Ukuqaphela inqubekelaphambili yephrojekthi kanye nokuhlangana njalo nababambe iqhaza namalunga ethimba lephrojekthi ukulinganisa nokubheka amaphuzu ahambisana nesikhathi . Amaphuzu amaningi angabangela ukuthi kuguquke isikhathi esibekelwe iphrojekthi . Okungenziwa uma uma kungaphumeleli kumele kufakwe kwishedula yokuqala futhi inqubekelaphambili kumele yaziswe bonke ababambe iqhaza . Izinga lemfundo leningi lezingane zabantu abamnyama liphansi . Lokhu kuncisha iningi labafundi amathuba okuthola imisebenzi . Kuphinde kunciphise amandla okuhola nokukhula ngokomsebenzi kulabo abathola imisebenzi - futhi kunciphisa amandla okuguquka kwamabhizinisi aseNingizimu Afrika . Ishedula lezinsizakusebenza liyoqukatha imisebenzi eyafakwa kwishedula yomsebenzi , lokhu kumele kuphinde kubhalwe konke nazo zonke izidingo kanye nezindleko ukuqinisekisa impumelelo yakho .
